Ms. Yuxi Zhang's team won the Best Data Challenge Poster at NetMob 2025!

October 09, 2025

Ms. Yuxi Zhang’s team from Shibuya lab (Yuxi Zhang, Kanata Takahashi, Sijian Tian, Hibiki Sumioku, and Yuya Shibuya) won the Best Data Challenge Poster Award at NetMob 2025. Congrats!

Paper Information:
Title: Beyond Willpower: Structural Constraints and Inequality in Mobility-Related CO2 Emissions
Authors: Yuxi Zhang, Kanata Takahashi, Sijian Tian, Hibiki Sumioku, and Yuya Shibuya
Conference HP: http://netmob.org
